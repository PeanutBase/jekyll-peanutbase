About PeanutBase

**Work in Progress: Change the text**

PeanutBase integrates genetic and genomic information across many Arachis species including cultivated peanut. Access this information by selecting a genus under TAXA, or a tool or method under TOOLS, or select DOWNLOAD via the Data Store. You can also explore most of the data via the rich toolsets at PeanutMine (also accessed throughout this website).

Taxa - Resources for legumes at various taxonomic levels (species, genera, and the family as a whole): data sets, genome browsers, pan-gene sets, synteny visualizations, sequence and keyword search, etc.

Tools - Pan-legume tools: sequence and keyword search, gene families and phylogenies, synteny visualization, etc. search, etc.

Download - Browse and download data sets from the LIS Data Store.
