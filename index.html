PeanutBase integrates genetic and genomic information across many Arachis species including cultivated peanut. See GENOMICS to access resources and tools such as browsers, organized by species and accession. See DOWNLOAD to access data sets for download. See TOOLS if you have particular tasks in mind. You can also explore most of the data via the rich toolsets at ArachisMine (also accessed throughout this website). Please contact us if you have questions, suggestions, or contributions.
