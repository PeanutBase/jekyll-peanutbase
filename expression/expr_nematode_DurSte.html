Arachis duranensis Gene Expression: Nematode

Click for a description of the table, and credits and usage agreement

A. duranensis X M. arenaria gene expression: days of infection vs. control (no infection) comparisons
From this table, one can easily obtain the number of differentially expressed genes (log2FC > 2 or < -2, FDR <0.05) between days of infection and control. For example, for the cell of 3days vs up (vs.control), the number 241 means there are 241 genes are up-regulated in this comparison. Clicking on the cell will result in a downloadable list of genes with their edgeR analysis results, including log2FoldChange (log2FC) and adjusted-pvalue (FDR). In addition, gene GBrowse, sequence link and functional annotation are also included for further exploration.

Credits: M. arenaria infected A. duranensis root transcriptome data was produced by Patricia Messenberg Guimar達es and colleagues.
Reference: Guimaraes PM, et al. (2015) Root Transcriptome Analysis of Wild Peanut Reveals Candidate Genes for Nematode Resistance. PLoS ONE 10(10): e0140937). (PubMed)

Usage agreement: Please contact the authors, or enquire via Contact us, before using or citing this data.
