About PeanutBase

PeanutBase is the community resource for the international peanut research and breeding community. PeanutBase is funded by the Peanut Foundation and the many donors to the International Peanut Genome Initiative. PeanutBase advisory committee

  • David Bertioli - University of Georgia
  • Kelly Chamberlin - USDA, Wheat, Peanut, and Other Field Crops Research, Stillwater, OK

  • Ray Schnell - Mars Corporation
